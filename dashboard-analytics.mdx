# Overview

The new Dashboard Analytics feature provides real-time insights into user behavior and system performance, allowing you to track key performance indicators (KPIs) effectively. This tool is designed to help you understand and optimize your content's impact.

Key features include:

- Real-time data visualization to monitor metrics as they happen.

- Customizable dashboard widgets to focus on the data that matters most to you.

- Export capabilities to download your data in CSV or PDF format.

- Automated reporting to receive scheduled insights directly in your email.

# How to Customize Your Dashboard

1\. Navigate to the **Analytics** section from the main menu.

2\. On the dashboard, click the **Add Widget** button to open the widget library.

3\. Select the widget you want to add by clicking on it. You can configure its settings, such as the specific KPI to track and the desired time frame.

4\. Once added, you can rearrange widgets by clicking and dragging them to your preferred position on the dashboard.

5\. Click the **Save Layout** button to save your customized dashboard view.

# How to Export Data

1\. From the Analytics Dashboard, locate the **Export** button, typically found in the top-right corner.

2\. Click the **Export** button to open the export options.

3\. A dialog window will appear. Select your desired file format: **CSV** or **PDF**.

4\. Click **Download** to begin the export. The file will be saved to your device.

# How to Schedule Email Reports

1\. On the Analytics Dashboard, click the **Schedule Reports** button.

2\. In the configuration window, set up your report preferences:

- **Recipients**: Enter the email addresses of those who should receive the report.

- **Frequency**: Choose how often the report should be sent (e.g., daily, weekly, or monthly).

- **Content**: Select which dashboard data to include in the report.

3\. Click **Save Schedule** to activate the automated reporting.
