When your product interface evolves, your documentation must follow suit without friction. Pageloop streamlines this process by allowing you to record a video flow of your product changes. We automatically extract the relevant text and media from your recording, giving you the exact assets you need to create new articles or update existing ones.

Whether you are [creating a new article using Flow](/pageloop-helpdesk/create-a-new-article-using-flow) or [finding updates](/pageloop-helpdesk/finding-updates-using-pageloop) for published content, the extracted video assets are readily available in your editor.

# Leverage media from your video flow

After capturing your screen actions, Pageloop populates your workspace with intelligent suggestions and a dedicated media library.

1. Open the editor for your new or updated article. On the right side of the interface, select the **Media** tab to view all screenshots automatically extracted from your video recording.

2. Switch to the **Updates** tab to review the specific changes Pageloop recommends based on your recorded actions.

3. To update an outdated image, click on a screenshot suggestion. In the replacement popover, select **Media from Flow** to instantly swap the old image with the accurate, up-to-date version captured in your video.

4. To provide additional visual context, simply drag and drop images directly from the Media sidebar into the appropriate section of your article.

5. Finally, review the AI-generated text suggestions. Click **Accept** to integrate the precise, step-by-step instructions derived from your flow directly into your documentation.

# Next steps

Once you have applied your media and text updates, you are ready to finalize your content. Learn more about the publication process in our guide on [using Pageloop to maintain your knowledge base](/pageloop-helpdesk/using-pageloop-to-maintain-your-knowledge-base).
