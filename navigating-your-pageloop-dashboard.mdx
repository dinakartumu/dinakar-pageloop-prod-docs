# Navigating Your Pageloop Dashboard

Welcome to Pageloop! Your dashboard is the central hub for managing your knowledge base content. This guide will walk you through the main sections you'll see when you first log in: Home and Drafts.

## Home

When you first log into Pageloop, you'll land on the **Home** page. This page is designed to help you get started quickly. You will find a Setup Checklist to guide you through the initial configuration and options to integrate your existing help center.

## Drafts

To view and manage your articles, click on the **Drafts** option in the left-hand navigation sidebar.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 1](../assets/ecba6f054111-upload12588277221598244786.png)
</Frame>

The Drafts section displays a list of all your articles. Here you can see important information at a glance, including:

- **Article Title:** The title of your document.

- **Status:** The current state of the article (e.g., completed).

- **Created By:** The team member who created the draft.

- **Created At:** The date and time the draft was created.

You can also use the search and filter options at the top to find specific articles quickly.
