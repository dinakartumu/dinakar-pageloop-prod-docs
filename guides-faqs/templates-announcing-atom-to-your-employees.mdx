# 2-week employee communication journey plan

To set your employees up for success, consider a 2-week communication plan that will help employees get familiar with Atom's capabilities and realize small wins. Please work with your onboarding lead to plan what capabilities you'd like to highlight for your employees to realize your deflection goals.

1. **Usecase 1: Look for information**

   Hello! I’m Atom \[Assistant name] and I can help you get what you need fast so you have time to focus on important tasks!

   Here are some questions you can ask me:

   - "What is the WiFi password?"

   - "How do I change my VPN gateway?"

   - "Where can I find sales enablement collateral?"

2. **Usecase 2: Create a request and get status**

   Hello! Did you know that getting help can be really really straightforward? All you have to do is:

   - Ask me a question like “How do I get a new laptop?”

   - Click on “Get help”

   - Fill out the form

   - And voila! A ticket will be created in your name and logged.

3. **Usecase 3: Limitations**

   As your AI Assistant, I'm here to streamline your work and provide quick answers. However, I do have a few limitations.

   I can access your organization’s data but I can’t surf the Internet like ChatGPT can so I might not be able to answer every question you have. I have also been trained to protect your data so I won’t store your personal information or pass it on to someone else.

   Curious about what else I can do? Ask me “What can you do?”

4. **Usecase 4: Try a skill - Ask about app status**

   Picture this: getting instant answers for everyday questions.

   Test me with, “Is Zoom up?” to get a status update for Zoom

5. **Usecase 5: Try a skill - Get added to a DL**

   Need to join a distribution list? Just tell me which one and experience how simple it is to stay connected. Try it out now by saying 'Add me to the Atom Fans list'.
