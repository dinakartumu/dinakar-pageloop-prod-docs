# Navigating the Pageloop Dashboard

Welcome to Pageloop! Your dashboard is the central hub for managing your knowledge base and content. This guide will walk you through the main sections to help you get started and find your way around.

## The Home Dashboard

When you first log in to Pageloop, you will land on the Home dashboard. This page is designed to guide you through the initial setup process.

On this page, you will find:

- **Setup Checklist:** Track your progress in setting up your Pageloop account.

- **Integrate your help center:** Connect to your knowledge base to allow Pageloop to scan for issues and suggest improvements.

The main navigation panel is located on the left side, providing access to all key areas of the application.

## The Drafts Page

To view and manage your articles, click on **Drafts** in the left-hand navigation panel.

The Drafts page is where all your articles are stored. From this screen, you can:

- See a list of all your articles, along with their status, creator, and creation date.

- Start a new article by clicking the **Create Article** button in the top-right corner.
