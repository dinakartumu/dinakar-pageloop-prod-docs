We are committed to maintaining a safe and respectful environment for our community. If you encounter a petition or comment that violates our Terms of Service or Community Guidelines, you can report it directly to our team for review.

# Reporting a petition

If you believe a petition infringes on platform policies, you can flag it directly from the petition page.

1. <Frame>
     [![asdasdHello213](../assets/737dc8da57b1-1767969038093-db719201.png)](https://ddinalar.comasd)
   </Frame>

   Scroll to the bottom of the petition's main text and click on the **Report a policy violation** link.

2. A **Report abuse** dialog will appear. Select the reason that best describes the violation, such as "Misleading or spam" or "Abusive or hateful."

3. Provide a detailed explanation in the text box to assist our staff in reviewing the content.

4. Click **Submit**.

# Reporting a comment

You can also flag individual comments that appear under a petition.

1. Scroll down to the **Supporter Voices** section to view comments from other users.

2. Locate the specific comment you wish to report and click the **Report** button next to it.

3. In the dialog box that appears, choose the reason for reporting the comment and provide any necessary context.

4. Click **Submit** to finalize your report.

# Next steps

Once submitted, your report is sent to our team for review. We assess all flagged content against our Community Guidelines to determine the appropriate action.
