What you see in Pageloop After you sign in, Pageloop opens to the Home dashboard. The Home dashboard includes a Welcome header and an Overview section. Use the left navigation to move between: Home (your dashboard overview), Drafts (AI-generated articles awaiting review), Updates (articles flagged as needing changes), and Suggestions (AI-recommended improvements). The Overview highlights key work items, such as draft articles and articles that need updates.

**Note:** On the Home dashboard, you may see an **Add to Chrome** button if the Pageloop Chrome extension isn’t installed or connected yet.

What Pageloop can do Create draft articles from screen actions, screenshots, and product notes. Detect articles that may be out of date based on product changes and release notes. Propose rewrites in your brand

Where Pageloop works Pageloop integrates with common help center platforms, including Zendesk, Intercom, HubSpot, Freshdesk, and HelpJuice. How the Chrome extension fits in Use the Pageloop Chrome extension to capture steps while you use your product. Pageloop can then turn those steps into drafts and update suggestions.
