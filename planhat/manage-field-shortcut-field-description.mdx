title: Manage Field Shortcut & Field Description
description: Right click on column headings to access manage field
---

Field management is an essential part of using the data module in Planhat.

To make it quicker to access the field manager, you can now right click on any field (column) heading and select "Manage Field".

We've also added the "Description" section to the field manager, for **custom fields,&#x20;**&#x61;llowing your team to stay better aligned.

📣 **Quick tip:&#x20;**&#x75;se the "Description" for...

- the unabbreviated name of an abbreviated field

- the source of the data used to populate the field

- the name of the owner of the field

- how regularly the field is updated

- ...
