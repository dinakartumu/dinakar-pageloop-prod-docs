# Navigating the Pageloop UI

The Pageloop dashboard is designed for easy navigation, giving you quick access to all your key tools. This guide will walk you through the main sections available in the sidebar: Home, Drafts, and Updates. Dinakar

## Home

The **Home** page is your main dashboard and starting point. It provides a helpful 'Setup Checklist' to get you started with connecting your knowledge base and configuring your account. You can also manage your help center integrations directly from this page.

## Drafts

The **Drafts** section is where you can find all your articles that are currently in progress. This view allows you to see the status of each draft, who created it, and the creation date. You can also start a new article by clicking the 'Create Article' button.

<Frame>
  ![Screenshot 1](../assets/67038f0171f0-upload2190715878790477865.png)
</Frame>

## Updates

The **Updates** section helps you keep your knowledge base content fresh and accurate. Pageloop automatically detects potential changes for your published articles and lists them here. You can review details such as what triggered the update, when it was detected, and the number of pending changes for each article.
