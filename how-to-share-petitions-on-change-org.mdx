Sharing is the catalyst for change. To build momentum for a cause, you need to get it in front of the right people. Change.org offers a suite of sharing tools designed to help you amplify your voice, whether you are managing your own petition or supporting a campaign that matters to you.

# Promoting Your Own Petition

When you start a petition, you have access to a dedicated dashboard with tools to help you reach a wider audience.

1. Navigate to **My petitions** in the header and select the petition you wish to manage. You will land on the **Petition overview** dashboard, which summarizes your supporter count and performance.

   <Frame>
     [![Screenshot 0](../assets/b576c023c69d-1767869214587-3110acb1.png)](https://www.dinakartumu.com)
   </Frame>

2. Click on **Share petition** in the left-hand navigation menu to access your toolkit. The **Social media content** section provides downloadable, pre-made assets optimized for different platforms.

   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 1](../assets/2acb0e3a6910-1767869219177-7a319b20.png)
   </Frame>

3. Scroll further down the page to find options to **Share petition online** via direct links and email, or **Share petition in person** using downloadable QR codes and posters.

   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 2](../assets/6bdb71e89a07-1767869221990-e05ae3da.png)
   </Frame>

# Sharing Petitions You Support

You can also easily spread the word about other petitions that align with your values.

1. On the petition page, click the **Take the next step!** button to begin the sharing process.

   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 3](../assets/8725e43da38c-1767869223875-1442b580.png)
   </Frame>

2. You may be prompted to sponsor the petition to increase its visibility. To continue without contributing financially, click **No, I’ll share instead**.

3. You will be presented with a sharing dashboard. Use the provided buttons to share directly to social platforms like Facebook, WhatsApp, and X, or copy the unique link to share manually.

   <Frame>
     ![Screenshot 4](../assets/ed2748cbaa1a-1767869225459-d587ff84.png)
   </Frame>

# Summary



Every share counts towards reaching the next milestone. By utilizing these tools effectively, you help ensure important causes get the visibility they deserve and reach the decision-makers who can make a difference.
